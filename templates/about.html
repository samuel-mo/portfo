03 : About me

Education

Graduated from the United States Military Academy at West Point in 2016 with my BS in Systems Engineering. I am fluent in Mandarin-Chinese. I have completed the ZTM Python Developer course.

Work Experience

I commissioned into the US Army as a 2LT in 2016 as a Field Artillery Officer. I served in the 101st Airborne Division at Fort Campbell, KY from 2017-2020 and with the Special Warfare Training Group at Fort Bragg, NC from 2020-2022. A link to my résumé can be found here.

Hobbies

I am a woodworker/maker in my free time. I enjoy tinkering and making things using design software such as Lightburn and Vectric Aspire on my CO2 Laser and computer numerical control (CNC) router, respectivly. My wife and I have three dogs and we love competing in dock diving, agility, and UpDog frisbee challenge with them.
